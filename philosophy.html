OUR PHILOSOPHY

Where data shapes every allocation.

Our Principals

Qurve baskets are ready to invest portfolios of mutual funds, built and managed using quantitative models. We select the right funds, allocate with precision, and rebalance based on data so your portfolio stays aligned with market conditions at all times.

Principle

Baskets Follow Data, Not Emotions

Baskets Follow Data, Not Emotions: Your baskets track quantitative signals across market cycles including momentum, valuations, and sector strength. Every allocation decision lives in the data. No guesswork. No commissions driving the call.

Principle

Baskets Rebalance Systematically

Baskets Rebalance Systematically: When market conditions shift, your baskets respond. They move to cash when the data says so. They rotate into multi asset funds when that makes sense. They tell you exactly what changed and why, every time.

Principle

Baskets Built for Market Cycles

Baskets Built for Market Cycles: Markets have seasons. Your baskets are designed to recognize them including expansion, correction, and recovery. By adapting to where we are in the cycle, the baskets aim for better risk adjusted returns, not just returns.

Principle

Transparency Over Mystique

Transparency Over Mystique: You see the logic behind every move. When a basket rebalances, you get clarity on what triggered it and the reasoning behind it. Trust comes from knowing exactly how your money is being deployed.

Principle

Capital Protection Built In

Capital Protection Built In: The baskets do not chase everything. They systematically balance risk across holdings, sectors, and asset classes. Downside protection is not an afterthought. It is woven into how the basket operates, so growth does not come at the cost of volatility you cannot stomach.

How It Works

Step 1

Step 1: Schedule and Share Book a call with us and fill out a quick form. This helps us understand you before we connect.

Step 2

Step 2: Understand Your Goals During our call, we discuss your financial aspirations and which basket or fund portfolio aligns with what you want to achieve.

Step 3

Step 3: Complete KYC A simple 15 minute verification step. We collect the documents we need to set up your account properly.

Step 4

Step 4: Authorize and Invest We set up your investment as either a one time lump sum or a monthly SIP, depending on what works for you. You authorize it through your bank using internet banking or UPI. Easy and secure.

Step 5

Step 5: Units Get Allocated Within 4 to 5 days, your mutual fund units are allocated to your portfolio. You receive login credentials and can download our app to view everything.

Step 6

Step 6: We Monitor, You Relax We continuously track your portfolio and rebalance when needed. If you ever want to withdraw, your money goes back to your bank account within 2 to 3 days.
