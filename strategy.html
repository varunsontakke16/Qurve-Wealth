Qurve All Weather Basket

A diversified basket designed to stay resilient when markets get unpredictable. For investors who value stability as much as growth.

This basket is built to perform across market cycles—whether equities are rising, falling, or moving sideways. By combining equity, debt, and other diversifying assets, it aims to reduce volatility while still capturing growth opportunities.

The allocation dynamically balances risk and return, helping cushion downside during market stress while participating in upside during favorable conditions. It’s ideal for investors who prefer consistency over extremes and want a “set-and-stay” core portfolio.

Best suited for: Investors seeking long-term stability, moderate growth, and reduced portfolio swings without constantly tracking markets.

Typical composition: 5–6 carefully selected schemes across asset classes (equity, debt, hybrid, possibly gold or alternatives).

2-3 schemes

Qurve Smart Debt Basket

Steady returns without the stock market rollercoaster. For investors who want reliable income and capital preservation built into their portfolio.

This basket focuses on high-quality debt instruments designed to generate stable and predictable returns. It emphasizes capital preservation, liquidity, and income generation, making it less sensitive to equity market volatility.

Through a mix of short-duration, corporate bond, and dynamic bond funds, it adapts to interest rate cycles while maintaining a strong risk framework. The goal is to deliver better-than-savings returns with controlled downside risk.

Best suited for: Conservative investors, retirees, or anyone looking to park funds with relatively low risk while earning consistent income.

Typical composition: 2–3 debt-oriented schemes with strong credit quality and duration management.

4-6 schemes

Qurve Growth Basket

Pure Equity, Built on data. For investors ready to grow wealth through disciplined, tactical allocation over the long term.

This basket is designed for long-term wealth creation through disciplined exposure to equities. It uses a structured, data-driven approach to allocate across market caps, sectors, and styles—aiming to capture growth while managing concentration risk.

Rather than chasing trends, the strategy focuses on consistency, rebalancing, and tactical allocation to adapt to evolving market conditions. Over time, this approach seeks to compound wealth while smoothing out the impact of market cycles.

Best suited for: Investors with a long-term horizon who are comfortable with market fluctuations and focused on maximizing capital appreciation.

Typical composition: 4–6 equity schemes diversified across large-cap, mid-cap, flexi-cap, and thematic opportunities.
